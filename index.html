Salt Roasted Pecans

2 Cups of Pecans

2Tbs. butter melted

1 1/4 tsp. fine sea salt

Preparation

Preheat oven to 325. Toss 2 cups raw pecan halves and 3 Tbsp. Unsalted butter, melted, in a medium bowl to coat; sprinkle with 1 1/4 tsp. fine sea salt, then toss again. Spread out pecans on a rimmed baking sheet and bake, tossing halfway through, until fragrant and slightly darkened, 12-15 minutes. Let cool on baking sheet.

Dried Beef Sticks

5lbs. ground beef

5tbs. quick salt

3 tsp. mustard seed

2 tsp. garlic

2 tsp. cracked pepper

1 Tbs. Smoked Salt

1 Tbs. liquid smoke

Preparation

Mix beef and season. Place in a large covered container for three days. On third day, shape beef into sticks, and bake at 150 for 8 hours (turn half-way through).
